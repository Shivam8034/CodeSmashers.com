Responsive Blogging Website

A blog (a truncation of "weblog")[1] is a discussion or informational website published on the World Wide Web consisting of discrete, often informal diary-style text entries (posts). Posts are typically displayed in reverse chronological order so that the most recent post appears first, at the top of the web page. The rise of Twitter and other "microblogging" systems helps integrate MABs and single-author blogs into the news media.
